~~ About Me ~~

Hello World!

My name is Robert, I'm an online degree student taking courses from Brigham Young University-Idaho.
I'm currently working on the Computer Programming Certificate. I've learned quite a bit already, but there's still much more to learn.
Here are a few key things about myself that I don't mind sharing with you:

I love to be creative

I love to build levels on Super Mario Maker 2 on the Nintendo Switch and watch twitch streamers suffer... err... enjoy the challenges they bring.
Other things I've used my creative mind on include poetry and backstories for Roleplay characters on Final Fantasy XIV.
Overall, I just enjoy using my creative mind anyway I can, and hope to someday find a career where I can use it to earn an income.

Pizza Lover

The first essay I had to write in the English course during Pathways was a love letter to a thing I love. It couldn't be a person, or a place.
So, I wrote a love letter to pizza (ask and maybe I'll let you read it). The pizza in my picture on the home page is a 24-inch New York Style from Mario's Pizza.

Disability

I have Tourette's Syndrome and Autism Spectrum Disorder, which comes with Anxiety, Depression, OCD, and some learning disabilities.
So I ask to be patient with me if I'm a little slow at times. Because of my disability, I never imagined myself here. Thanks to the church and the Pathway program, here I am. For with God nothing shall be impossible.
